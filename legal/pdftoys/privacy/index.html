Privacy Policy for PDF Toys

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gemiry Company Limited ("Gemiry," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with PDF Toys (the "App"), an app for working with PDF files. We built PDF Toys with privacy in mind.

1. We do not collect your information or files

PDF Toys does not collect, store, or share your personal information. We do not ask you to create an account, and the App does not gather your name, files, or other personal data to send to us.

The PDF files you work with are processed locally on your device and saved where you choose. They are never uploaded to our servers or shared with us or any third party.

2. Local operation

All PDF processing happens on your device. Any settings or preferences you configure are kept on your device. The App does not transmit your files or data to our servers or to any third party.

3. No analytics, tracking, or ads

We do not use analytics or tracking, we do not show ads, and we do not sell or share your data. There is no profile or identifier associated with you.

4. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

6. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@gemiry.com.
