About us

Thoughtful software, made with care.

We're Gemiry — a small, independent team that loves building apps. We believe technology is at its best when it gets out of the way: when it's clear, calm, and genuinely helpful.

Every product we make starts from the same question — how do we make this simpler? We obsess over the small details, polish the rough edges, and design for real people, not power users. The result is software that feels good to use from the very first tap.
