Privacy Policy for MonitorSync

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gemiry Company Limited ("Gemiry," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with MonitorSync (the "App"). We built MonitorSync with privacy in mind.

1. We do not collect your personal information

MonitorSync does not collect, store, or share your personal information. We do not ask you to create an account, and the App does not gather your name, files, or other personal data to send to us. The App works on your device.

2. Third-party statistics

MonitorSync may include a third-party analytics or diagnostics service that automatically logs anonymous statistics for reporting and improvement purposes only, such as:

  • Usage statistics — anonymous information about how features are used and how the App performs;
  • Diagnostic data — app event and error logs, and basic technical details such as device type, operating system version, and app version.

This information is aggregated and used solely to understand stability and improve the App. It is not used to identify you personally, and we do not use it for advertising. The data handled by this service is subject to the relevant provider's own privacy policy.

3. We do not sell or share your data

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with third parties except for the limited, anonymous statistics described above, or where required by law.

4. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

6. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@gemiry.com.
