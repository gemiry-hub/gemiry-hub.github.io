Privacy Policy for Authik

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gemiry Company Limited ("Gemiry," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with Authik (the "App"), a two-factor authentication (2FA) app that generates one-time passwords (OTPs). Authik is built to keep your security data private and entirely on your device.

1. We do not collect your information

Authik does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not ask you to create an account, and the App does not transmit your data to us or to anyone else. The App works fully on your device.

2. Your 2FA keys stay on your device

The secret keys you add to Authik are stored encrypted, locally on your device. They are never sent to our servers or to any third party. One-time passwords (OTPs) are generated on your device and do not require an internet connection.

3. Camera and QR codes

If you use the App's camera to scan a QR code when adding an account, the image is processed on your device only. Authik does not store photos or transmit camera images anywhere.

4. No analytics, tracking, or ads

We do not use analytics or tracking, we do not show ads, and we do not sell or share your data. There is no profile or identifier associated with you.

5. Your responsibility for backups

Because your keys are stored only on your device, you are responsible for keeping access to them. If you use any backup or export feature provided by the App or your operating system, please keep those backups secure.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@gemiry.com.
