Privacy Policy for Snipik

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gemiry Company Limited ("Gemiry," "we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with Snipik (the "App"). We built Snipik with privacy in mind.

1. We do not collect your personal information

Snipik does not collect, store, or share your personal information by itself. We do not ask you to create an account, and the App does not gather your name, contacts, photos, files, or other personal data to send to us. Your content stays on your device.

2. Third-party SDKs

Snipik may include third-party software development kits (SDKs) — for example, crash reporting and diagnostics tools provided by the platform or other vendors. These SDKs may automatically collect limited technical information for reporting and stability purposes only, such as:

  • Diagnostic and crash logs — app event and error logs that help identify and fix problems;
  • Device information — technical details such as device model, operating system version, and app version.

This information is used solely to monitor stability, diagnose crashes, and improve the App. It is not used to identify you personally, and we do not use it for advertising. The data handled by these SDKs is subject to the relevant provider's own privacy policy.

3. We do not sell or share your data

We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with third parties except for the limited, report-only diagnostic data described above, or where required by law.

4. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.

6. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@gemiry.com.
